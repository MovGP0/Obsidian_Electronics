# Spintronics

This folder collects spintronic device notes and compact-model references for magnetic tunnel junction (MTJ) circuit simulation.

- [[UMN MTJ SPICE Models]]

- [[VCMA MTJ SPICE Model]]
- [[MTJ Compact Model Equations]]

## Downloaded archives

The source archives were downloaded from the University of Minnesota MTJ SPICE model site into `.temp/` and expanded there:

- `.temp/STT_model.zip`
- `.temp/SHE_model.zip`
- `.temp/VCMA_model.zip`

The STT archive contains three nested model archives that were also expanded: `IMTJ`, `cPMTJ`, and `iPMTJ`.
